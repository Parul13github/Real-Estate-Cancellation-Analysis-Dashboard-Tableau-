# Real-Estate-Cancellation-Analysis-Dashboard-Tableau-
Tableau dashboard analyzing real estate cancellations using cohort analysis, customer segmentation, and project-level insights to identify trends and revenue impact.


## Project Overview
This project is a **Tableau dashboard** built to analyze customer cancellations in a real estate business.

It was developed as an **extension of the main sales dashboard**, based on a client requirement to deeply understand **cancellation trends, customer behavior, and revenue impact**.

The dashboard provides a **360° view of cancellations** across projects, time, sales channels, and customer segments.

---

## Business Problem
Cancellations in real estate directly impact:
- Revenue forecasting  
- Inventory planning  
- Sales performance  

The client lacked visibility into:
- Cancellation trends over time  
- High-risk projects and sales channels  
- Customer segments contributing to cancellations  
- Retention and drop-off patterns after booking  

This dashboard helps convert raw data into **actionable insights for better decision-making**.

---

## Dashboard Views

### 1. Summary View
- Total Cancellation Value (TCP)
- Total Cancellation Count
- Average Rebooking Time (Days)
- Cancellation Trend by Project Type (Core vs Flagship)
- Cancellation by Sales Group
- Cancellation by Booking Time  

 *Purpose:* Quick high-level understanding of cancellation performance.

---

### 2. Table View (Detailed Analysis)
- Project-level breakdown:
  - Gross Sales vs Cancellation Volume  
  - Cancellation Value  
  - Cancellation Rate (%)  
- Sales Group Analysis:
  - Channel-wise performance  
  - Payment term impact  

 *Purpose:* Deep dive into project and operational insights.

---

### 3. Cohort View (Customer Retention Analysis)
- Tracks customer behavior from **Month 0 to Month 18+**
- Displays retention % vs cancellations over time  

 *Key Insight:*  
- Highest drop-offs occur in early months  
- Long-term retention trends become visible  

---

### 4. Customer View (Segmentation Analysis)
- Demographics:
  - Gender, Age, Marital Status  
  - Nationality, Country, City  
- Behavioral attributes:
  - Occupation, Employer  
  - Payment Scheme  
  - Unit Type & Project  

 *Purpose:* Identify **high-risk customer segments**.

---

## Key Insights
- Cancellation trend increased significantly after 2020  
- Different project types show varying cancellation risks  
- Average rebooking time is high (~358 days), impacting revenue flow  
- Certain customer segments contribute more to cancellations  
- Maximum cancellations occur in early booking months
